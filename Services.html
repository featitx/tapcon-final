Services Description

Calibration

Ensure the accuracy and reliability of your pharmaceutical processes with our comprehensive calibration services tailored for the unique needs of the pharma industry. At Tapcon, we specialize in providing precision calibration solutions that adhere to the stringent regulatory requirements and quality standards governing the pharmaceutical sector.

Our Services Include:

  • Instrument Calibration
  • Analytical Instrument Calibration
  • Environmental Control Systems Calibration
  • pH Meters and Electrochemical Instrument Calibration
  • Weighing and Balancing Equipment Calibration
  • Process Control Instrument Calibration
  • Regulatory Compliance Documentation

PLC Validation

Elevate the reliability and compliance of your pharmaceutical automation systems with our PLC (Programmable Logic Controller) validation services. Our team of skilled engineers and validation experts specializes in the validation of PLC systems used in pharmaceutical manufacturing, ensuring they meet the highest standards of precision, reliability, and regulatory compliance

Our Services Include:

  • Installation Qualification(IQ)
  • Operational Qualification (OQ)
  • Performance Qualification (PQ)
  • Change Control Management
  • Risk Assessment and Mitigation

Consultation

Unlock efficiency, reliability, and compliance in your pharmaceutical manufacturing processes with our specialized consultancy services at Tapcon. We provide comprehensive guidance on the selection, validation, and optimization of pharmaceutical machines and equipment to ensure seamless operations and adherence to global regulatory standards.

Our Services Include:

  • Equipment Selection and Procurement
  • Regulatory Compliance and Validation
  • Process Optimization
  • Technology Integration
  • Training Programs
  • Cost Optimization
